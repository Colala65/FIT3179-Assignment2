Global Meat Production and Consumption

Changes in the production and consumption of meat.



People from countries with higher GDP per capita tend to consume more meat.

In 2017, Australia and the United States have the two highest consumptions of meat per person.



Beginning from 1990, Pig meat is the most consumed type of meat. Going into 2017, Poultry eventually surpasses Pig meat as the most consumed.

Years starting from 2021 and onwards are projections.
